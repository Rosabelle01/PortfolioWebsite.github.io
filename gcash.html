DATA CLEANING USING EXCEL

I download the raw file of the GCash transaction history using the GCash app, and then they send it to my email.

I cleaned the data by first analyzing the needed data or column from the excel file. I used the different commands in Excel, like auto-sum to get the total sum of profits, ifs condition to get the cash in cash out profit based on their amount, sort, filter, format painter, and text to column to separate the cells, and removed all non-useful data.
